# Dome Research Squarespace Build Notes

Use this static mockup as the source of truth for layout and copy:

- `index.html`: homepage structure and copy
- `blog.html`: blog index structure and copy
- `styles.css`: visual direction, colors, spacing, and typography

## Recommended Squarespace Pages

1. Home
2. Blog

## Homepage Sections

1. Hero
   - Heading: Dome Research
   - Eyebrow: Investments | Incubations | Research
   - Body: We invest in durable ideas, incubate new companies, and publish research on markets, technology, and emerging business models.
   - Buttons: View Work, Read Research

2. Firm
   - Heading: Research-led capital and company formation.
   - Body: Dome Research combines independent analysis with hands-on operating support. The firm studies markets, allocates capital, and helps build early-stage ideas from thesis to launch.

3. Team
   - Add one bio card per person.
   - Keep each bio to 2-4 sentences.

4. Current Investments
   - Add one row per current investment.
   - Use: name, one-line description, optional link.

5. Strategies
   - Public and Private Markets
   - Venture Formation
   - Applied Research

6. Incubations
   - Add one card per active incubation.
   - Use: name, market, current stage, optional link.

7. Contact
   - Use `hello@domeresearch.xyz` unless you prefer a different inbox.

## Blog Setup

Create a Squarespace Blog Collection named `Blog`. Use the `blog.html` page as the visual model for the index:

- Date
- Post title
- Short summary

Suggested categories:

- Research
- Investments
- Incubations
- Strategy
